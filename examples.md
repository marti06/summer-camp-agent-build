# Good topics to try

Crowd-source one from the room, or pick from these. The best demo topics are
specific, current, and have a clear audience.

- best resources for shipping AI agents in 2026
- learning Rust coming from TypeScript
- self-hosting your own analytics (privacy-first)
- prompt engineering for production, not demos
- the state of local-first software
- accessibility for web apps: the practical starting kit
- founder-led sales for technical founders
- design systems for small teams

Tips:
- Specific beats broad: "learning Rust coming from TypeScript" > "Rust".
- Pick something the room cares about, let them shout it.
- A topic you know well makes it easy to sanity-check the agent's picks live.
